About Pacific Trails Resort

Our mission at Pacific Trails Resort is to get people back into nature and back in tune with themselves. Our resort provides a getaway that combines the raw beauty of the Big Sur landscape with luxury. Whether you're looking to be active and explore or unwind and relax, you will find that our resort can cover your every need.

Meet Our Team

Lisa Reyez

Founder

Lisa came to found Pacific Trails Resort in 1982 after her own transformative experience visiting the Big Sur wilderness. She heads the Pacific Trails team in bringing guests the ultimate relaxation and luxury getaway vacation.

Tess Martin

Yoga Instructor

Tess has been with Pacific Trails for over 10 years leading yoga classes in different styles, including Hatha, Vinyasa, Ashtanga, Yin, and more. She uses her practice to increase wellbeing and peace through the mind-body connection.

Martin Killarney

Kayak Instructor

Martin leads kayaking trips out onto the beautiful Pacific Ocean where guests can experience wonder from another perspective. He brings over 15 years of experience along with CPR/AED and First Aid certifications.

Justin Mendoza

Guest Experience

Justin acts as the main liaison for guests and their needs here at the Resort. As a local turned a permanent resident of the Resort, he treats this place as his home where visitors are family.
